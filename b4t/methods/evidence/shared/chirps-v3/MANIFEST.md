Verified claims supported by saved artifacts:

- CHIRPS v3 is a quasi-global land precipitation product spanning `60N` to `60S`
- CHIRPS v3 has `0.05` degree spatial resolution
- CHIRPS v3 covers `1981` to near-real time
- CHIRPS v3 combines climatology, thermal infrared satellite observations, and station observations
- CHIRPS v3 provides multi-month products, including `3-monthly` global outputs
- CHIRPS v3 adds more station data sources, gauge-undercatch correction, and improved variance representation relative to v2

Unresolved gaps still blocking a direct CRI replacement decision:

- no saved source yet proves a like-for-like precomputed CRI drought-event frequency layer
- no saved source yet proves the exact SPI climatology or event-derivation baseline needed for CRI equivalence
- no saved source yet resolves whether `60N` to `60S` coverage is sufficient for the intended CRI footprint
