## Brief

### Background & rationale

The MELIAF Climate Adaptation Activator is a CGIAR initiative that builds a next-generation system for tracking and communicating CGIAR's contributions to climate adaptation. It establishes a common logic for how adaptation interventions connect to climate challenges and deliver results, and upgrades existing institutional adaptation planning, assessment, and reporting processes with clear guidance, robust data standards, and smarter automation.

The Activator is not a new standalone platform; it builds on and aligns existing systems — such as PRMS, program planning templates, and MELIAF frameworks — to capture adaptation-relevant information more effectively. It draws on evidence and best practices from CGIAR's Adaptation Insights project as the scientific backbone for operationalizing adaptation tracking across CGIAR.

Target users include CGIAR Programs, MELIA specialists, and CGIAR System-level units responsible for planning, performance reporting, and engagement with global and national adaptation processes (e.g. UNFCCC, Global Goal on Adaptation).

The scope of CDH support is to be explored in an upcoming conversation with Andreea Nowak. Potential areas include providing global climate data and intermediate products, expert review of proposed methodologies, or co-development of methods.
