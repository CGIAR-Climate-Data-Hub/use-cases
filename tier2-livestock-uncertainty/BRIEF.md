## Brief

### Background & rationale

CGIAR (Alliance Bioversity & CIAT) has been developing an emissions uncertainty calculator for livestock methane emissions, primarily focused on CH₄ from enteric fermentation in cattle, in collaboration with the Global Methane Hub (GMH) and UN Environment. This work aims to improve the accuracy and reliability of Tier 2 GHG inventory estimates for livestock systems.

There is an opportunity to apply this calculator to national GHG inventories in Colombia and possibly Nigeria, two countries with significant livestock sectors. The scope of CDH involvement is yet to be defined.
